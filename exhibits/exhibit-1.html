Exhibit No. 1 – Good Morning Houston, 1992

A quiet studio moment that became one of his proudest memories

This photograph comes from an appearance with Dolly Parton on Good Morning Houston. The original print is dated March 10, 1992 in Raymond’s handwriting in the white space below the image. This version has been digitally cleaned and restored from that original photograph.
